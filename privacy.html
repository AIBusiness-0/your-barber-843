4. SMS Messaging Program

By opting in via our web form, you consent to receive SMS text messages from Your Barber 843 (+1 843-203-5073). Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for assistance.

Your Barber 843 will never sell, rent, trade, or share your mobile phone number or SMS opt-in information with any third party for their marketing or promotional purposes. Your contact information is used solely to communicate with you about your appointments and our services.

6. Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use cookies, web beacons, pixels, or any third-party tracking technologies to collect information about your visit or browsing behavior. No tracking data is collected or stored.

7. Data Security Practices

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Your data is stored securely and access is restricted to authorized personnel only. We use industry-standard security protocols to safeguard your information. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information with service providers (such as our appointment booking platform, Perceny) solely to operate our business, under confidentiality agreements.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To unsubscribe from SMS messages, reply STOP to any message. For other requests, contact us at (843) 203-5073 or info@yourbarber843.com.

11. Age Requirement

Our SMS program is intended for individuals 18 years of age or older. By opting in, you confirm you are at least 18 years old.
