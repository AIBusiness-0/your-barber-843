Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Who We Are

Robyck LLC, doing business as Your Barber 843 ("we," "us," or "our"), is a barbershop located at 10150 Dorchester Rd Unit 226, Summerville, SC 29485. Phone: (843) 203-5073. Email: info@yourbarber843.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Name and mobile phone number (when you complete our opt-in form)
  • Appointment preferences and stylist selections
  • SMS consent records and opt-in timestamps

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To send transactional SMS messages including booking links, appointment confirmations, reminders, and missed call follow-ups
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • To improve our services

4. SMS Messaging Program

By optionally checking the SMS consent box on our form, you consent to receive transactional and informational SMS text messages from Robyck LLC dba Your Barber 843 (+1 843-203-5073). Message types include: booking links, appointment confirmations, appointment reminders, rescheduling updates, and missed call follow-ups. Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for assistance.

5. No Sharing of SMS Opt-In Data

Robyck LLC will never sell, rent, trade, or share your mobile phone number or SMS opt-in information with any third party for their marketing or promotional purposes. Your contact information is used solely to communicate with you about your appointments and our services.

6. Cookies, Tracking Technologies, and Analytics

This website does not use cookies, web beacons, pixels, Google Analytics, or any third-party tracking or analytics technologies. We do not collect any data about your browsing behavior, device, or visit patterns. No tracking data of any kind is collected, stored, or shared.

7. Data Security Measures

We take the following measures to protect your personal information:

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Access to customer information is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We do not store form submissions on this website — data is processed securely through our CRM platform (GoHighLevel), which uses industry-standard encryption and security practices
  • We regularly review our data handling practices to ensure ongoing security
  • In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you as required by applicable law

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information with service providers (such as our appointment booking platform, Perceny, and our CRM platform, GoHighLevel) solely to operate our business, under confidentiality agreements.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To unsubscribe from SMS messages, reply STOP to any message. For other requests, contact us at (843) 203-5073 or info@yourbarber843.com.

11. Age Requirement

Our SMS program is intended for individuals 18 years of age or older. By opting in, you confirm you are at least 18 years old.
